PCL-5 — Weathers et al., National Center for PTSD, 2013

The PCL-5 is the current gold standard PTSD screening tool, revised to align with the DSM-5 criteria. It measures the 20 PTSD symptoms across four clusters: Intrusion, Avoidance, Negative Alterations in Cognition and Mood, and Alterations in Arousal and Reactivity. In the creative industry, trauma exposure through high-pressure environments, public scrutiny, precarious work, and interpersonal conflict is frequently underrecognized.
