Sleep Quality Index

PSQI — Buysse, Reynolds, Monk, Berman & Kupfer, 1989

The PSQI is the gold standard clinical measure of sleep quality, covering 7 components: subjective sleep quality, sleep latency, sleep duration, sleep efficiency, sleep disturbances, use of sleeping medication, and daytime dysfunction. In the creative industry, disrupted sleep is endemic — driven by irregular schedules, deadline pressure, screen exposure, and the always-on culture of digital creative work.
