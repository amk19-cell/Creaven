The CAGE is one of the most widely used first-line clinical screening instruments for detecting possible alcohol dependence or problem drinking. Its four questions each represent a diagnostic dimension: Cut down, Annoyed, Guilty, Eye-opener. Simple, fast, and clinically validated in primary care settings worldwide.
