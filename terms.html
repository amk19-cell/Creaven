Description of services

Creaven provides the following services through its platform at creaven.pages.dev:

  • Psychological assessments: validated psychometric tools covering depression, anxiety, burnout, PTSD, sleep, self-esteem, addiction, and related dimensions.
  • Individual consultations: one-on-one remote sessions with licensed psychologists or psychosocial support specialists.
  • Organizational programs: custom well-being programs designed for creative companies, agencies, and studios.
  • AI-assisted communication: real-time interpretation support during sessions to bridge language barriers.

All services are delivered entirely remotely via secure digital channels. Creaven reserves the right to modify, suspend, or discontinue any service at any time with reasonable notice.

Nature of services - not emergency care

Creaven is not an emergency service. If you or someone you know is in immediate danger or experiencing a psychiatric emergency, please contact your local emergency services (police, ambulance, or crisis line) immediately.

The services provided by Creaven are intended for psychological support, well-being monitoring, and non-emergency mental health care. They do not constitute emergency psychiatric intervention, medical diagnosis, or treatment of clinical conditions requiring immediate intervention.

Creaven's emergency session feature provides access to a psychologist for urgent support but is not a substitute for emergency medical services. In the event of immediate risk to life, always contact local emergency services first.

Assessment results provided through the platform are intended as informational tools. They do not constitute a clinical diagnosis and should not be treated as such. All assessment results are reviewed by a licensed psychologist before delivery, but this does not replace a formal clinical evaluation.
