RSES — Morris Rosenberg, 1965

The Rosenberg Self-Esteem Scale is the gold standard 10-item measure of global self-esteem. One of the most widely validated and cited scales in social science research, it assesses both positive and negative feelings about oneself. In the creative industry, self-esteem is often tied to external validation, performance, and visibility — making it particularly vulnerable.
