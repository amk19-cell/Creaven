GAD-7 - Spitzer et al., 2006 | Primary Care Screening

The GAD-7 is a 7-item validated screening tool for generalized anxiety disorder - one of the most widely used anxiety measures in the world. Originally developed for primary care settings, it has since been validated for occupational health and is particularly useful for identifying anxiety related to workplace pressure, performance demands, and chronic stress.

Important notice: The GAD-7 is a validated screening tool, not a clinical diagnosis. A score suggesting moderate or severe anxiety does not mean you have an anxiety disorder - only a qualified mental health professional can make that determination.
