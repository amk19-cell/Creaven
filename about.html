About Creaven

The story behind the platform.

Not a pitch. Not a product page. This is why Creaven exists, who it is for, and what we refuse to compromise on.

Where this comes from
A problem hiding in plain sight.

The creative industry is one of the most demanding environments on earth. Not because it is harder than construction or medicine or agriculture. But because the suffering inside it is systematically invisible. The culture rewards performance. It punishes fragility. And it has spent decades telling the people inside it that exhaustion is the price of passion.

Most of the people who make culture, the ones you will never see on a stage or in a credits list, work in conditions of extreme pressure with near-total silence around mental health. When something breaks, the systems that exist were not built for them. Corporate mental health platforms require an employer who has opted in. Insurance plans require coverage. Local therapists require the right geography and the right language. And the cultural stigma in high-performance environments requires that you simply carry on.

Creaven was built because that gap is not a minor inconvenience. It costs careers, relationships, and lives. And no platform was built specifically to close it.

You do not have to be falling apart to deserve support. You just have to be human.
This is the single conviction at the center of everything Creaven builds. Not as a tagline. As a design principle. Every feature, every protocol, every clinical decision on this platform exists to make that sentence true in practice.

Who this is for
Every role. Without exception.

The creative industry is not just the faces you know. It is hundreds of professions, most of them invisible, all of them essential. Creaven is for all of them. If your work touches the creative world in any way, this platform was built with you in mind.

If your role is not listed here, it still counts. This list is a starting point, not a boundary. The creative industry is vast and constantly evolving. If you work in it, Creaven is for you.

The founder
G
Creaven was founded by Grace Daniella Amakon, a psychosocial support specialist with a Master's degree in social psychology and field experience in burnout prevention, psychosocial accompaniment, and mental health coordination through NGO work in West Africa. Creaven was not built from a boardroom or a startup incubator. It was built from years of observation, from fieldwork, and from a simple refusal to accept that the people who make culture should not have access to the same quality of psychological care as anyone else. The platform reflects both clinical rigor and lived understanding of what the creative industry actually demands from human beings.
