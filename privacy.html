01

Who we are and how to contact us

Creaven is a psychological well-being platform for workers in the creative and cultural industries, operated by Grace Daniella Amakon, based in Abidjan, Cote d'Ivoire. We are the data controller for all personal information collected through the Creaven platform at creaven.pages.dev.

For any privacy-related questions, requests, or concerns, please contact us at: creavenconnect@gmail.com

We aim to respond to all privacy-related requests within 5 business days.

02

What data we collect

We collect only the data necessary to provide our services. The following table describes what we collect, why, and how.

Category Data collected Source
Account data Full name, email address, account type, date of account creation Provided by you at registration
Session data Consultation date and time, session language, session code, psychologist assigned, notes shared before session Provided by you during booking
Assessment data Responses to psychometric assessments, scores, and psychologist review notes Generated during assessment completion
Payment data Transaction reference, amount paid, currency, payment method (card or mobile money), payment status Generated during payment via Paystack
Practitioner data Full name, date of birth, country of practice, professional languages, specialities, licence number, diploma and CV documents, LinkedIn profile Provided by practitioners during application
Technical data Browser type, device type, IP address, pages visited, session duration Collected automatically when you use the platform

We do not collect or store full payment card numbers. Card payments are processed entirely by Paystack, which is PCI-DSS compliant. Creaven only receives a transaction reference and status confirmation.

03

Why we collect your data

We collect and process your personal data for the following purposes:

  • Service delivery: To create and manage your account, process bookings, deliver assessments, and facilitate consultations with psychologists.
  • Payment processing: To process and confirm payments for assessments and consultations.
  • Clinical quality: To enable psychologists to review assessment results and provide informed, personalized support.
  • Communication: To send session confirmations, session codes, reminders, and responses to your inquiries.
  • Platform improvement: To understand how the platform is used in aggregate and improve our services. We do not use individual clinical data for this purpose.
  • Legal compliance: To comply with applicable laws and regulations in Cote d'Ivoire.

We do not sell your personal data. We do not use your data for advertising purposes. We do not share your data with employers, agencies, or any third party for commercial purposes.

04

How we store and protect your data

Your data is stored on Firebase (Google Cloud), a secure, encrypted cloud infrastructure operated by Google. Firebase uses industry-standard security measures including encryption at rest and in transit (TLS/HTTPS).

Access to your personal data within Creaven is strictly limited to:

  • The Creaven platform administrator (Grace Daniella Amakon)
  • The psychologist assigned to your session, who can only access data relevant to your consultations

Your assessment responses, session notes, and clinical data are never accessible to employers, organizations, or any party outside of your direct care relationship with your Creaven psychologist.

In the event of a data breach that poses a risk to your rights and freedoms, Creaven will notify affected users as soon as reasonably possible and take all necessary steps to contain and remediate the breach.

05

Who we share your data with

Creaven does not sell or rent your personal data. We share your data only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Your assigned psychologist: Your session details, pre-session notes, and relevant assessment results are shared with the psychologist conducting your consultation. This sharing is essential to delivering the service.
  • Paystack: Your email address and payment details are shared with Paystack to process your payment. Paystack's privacy policy applies to data processed on their platform.
  • Firebase (Google): Your data is stored on Google's Firebase infrastructure. Google does not have access to the content of your data for its own purposes under our service agreement.
  • Legal authorities: We may disclose your data if required to do so by law or in response to a valid legal request from competent authorities in Cote d'Ivoire.

We will never share your clinical information, assessment results, or session content with your employer, agency, record label, or any professional contact without your explicit written consent.

06

Data retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active and for a period of 3 years following your last activity on the platform. This retention period allows us to maintain continuity of care and respond to any queries or disputes that may arise.

After the retention period, your data will be permanently and irreversibly deleted from our systems.

You may request early deletion of your account and data at any time by contacting creavenconnect@gmail.com. Upon confirmed deletion, your data will be removed within 30 days, except where retention is required by applicable law.

Practitioner documents (diplomas and CVs) are retained for the duration of the practitioner's engagement with Creaven and deleted within 60 days of the end of that engagement.

07

Your rights

You have the following rights with respect to your personal data held by Creaven:

  • Right of access: You may request a copy of all personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to rectification: You may request that we correct any inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Right to erasure: You may request that we delete your personal data, subject to any legal retention obligations.
  • Right to portability: You may request that we provide your data in a structured, commonly used format.
  • Right to object: You may object to the processing of your data for purposes other than direct service delivery.
  • Right to withdraw consent: Where processing is based on consent, you may withdraw that consent at any time. Withdrawal of consent may require account closure.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at creavenconnect@gmail.com. We will respond within 5 business days and fulfill valid requests within 30 days.

08

Cookies and tracking

Creaven uses minimal technical cookies necessary for the platform to function, including session authentication cookies managed by Firebase. We do not use advertising cookies, third-party tracking pixels, or behavioral profiling tools.

We do not use Google Analytics or any equivalent third-party analytics service that would share your browsing behavior with external parties.

You can configure your browser to refuse cookies, but doing so may affect your ability to use certain features of the platform, including staying logged in to your account.

09

Children's privacy

Creaven's services are intended exclusively for adults aged 18 and over. We do not knowingly collect personal data from individuals under the age of 18.

If we become aware that we have inadvertently collected data from a person under 18, we will delete that data promptly. If you believe a minor has created an account on our platform, please contact us immediately at creavenconnect@gmail.com.

10

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, technology, legal requirements, or other factors. When we make material changes, we will notify registered users by email at least 14 days before the changes take effect.

The date of the most recent update is always displayed at the top of this page. Continued use of the Creaven platform after a policy update constitutes acceptance of the revised terms.

If you have questions about any changes to this policy, please contact us at creavenconnect@gmail.com before the effective date of the change.
