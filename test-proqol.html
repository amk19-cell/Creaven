Quality of Life Scale

ProQOL v5 — B. Hudnall Stamm, 2009–2012

The ProQOL is the most widely used measure of professional quality of life — the positive and negative effects of working in roles that involve helping others in some capacity. It measures three dimensions: Compassion Satisfaction (the positive dimension), Burnout, and Secondary Traumatic Stress. In the creative industry, these dynamics operate uniquely — through audience relationships, artistic exposure, service to clients, and the psychological demands of creative labor.
